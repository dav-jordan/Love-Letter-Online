RULES

At the beginning of each turn, the player draws a card and picks a card in their hand to discard. Something different happens depending on the card discarded. The different cards as well as their effects and the amount in the deck can be found below. The goal is to either be the last player standing or have the highest value out of all other players when the deck runs out. Princess(1): Lose if discarded Countess(1): Discard if you have king or prince in your hand King(1): Trade hands with another player Prince(2): Make one player discard their hand Handmaid(2): Protection until your next turn Baron(2): Compare hands, lower hand is out Priest(2): Look at someone else's hand Guard(5): Guess someone's hand. If correct, they are out.
